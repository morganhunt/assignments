A Trip Down Under

While Australia would be nice, New Zealand would be my ideal destination.

I would see scenes like they film in movies, plenty of rich forests and images worthy of being painted--if I could paint, anyway. I'll settle for photos.

I would love to see a kiwi, a bird that can't fly and has a ridiculously long beak to match its legs, but unfortunately this website doesn't have any pictures of them. I would definitely enjoy seeing a komodo dragon, though, even though I'm not sure they have any in New Zealand.

While I'm not sure what kind of food New Zealand is famous for, I'm certain an island would have wonderful seafood to try.
